# -- Double-click fires dblclick event --

===
no result before dblclick
===
plwr -S plwr-test text '#dblclick-result'
---


===
double-click the target
===
plwr -S plwr-test dblclick '#dblclick-target'
---

===

===
plwr -S plwr-test text '#dblclick-result'
---
double-clicked
