# -- Hover shows tooltip via CSS :hover --

===
tooltip hidden before hover
===
plwr -S plwr-test eval "getComputedStyle(document.getElementById('hover-tooltip')).display"
---
none

===
hover over the target
===
plwr -S plwr-test hover '#hover-me'
---

===
tooltip visible after hover
===
plwr -S plwr-test eval "getComputedStyle(document.getElementById('hover-tooltip')).display"
---
block
