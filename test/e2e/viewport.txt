===
navigate to test page
%require
===
plwr -S plwr-test open "http://localhost:8599/index.html"
---

===
set viewport to 800x600
===
plwr -S plwr-test viewport 800 600
---

===
viewport dimensions are reflected in JS
===
plwr -S plwr-test eval "window.innerWidth + 'x' + window.innerHeight"
---
800x600

===
change viewport to mobile size
===
plwr -S plwr-test viewport 375 667
---

===
mobile viewport dimensions
===
plwr -S plwr-test eval "window.innerWidth + 'x' + window.innerHeight"
---
375x667
